# Privacy Policy

**Metals Portfolio**

*Last updated: January 14, 2025*

## Overview

Metals Portfolio is a precious metals tracking app that helps you monitor your gold and silver holdings. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Data Collection

Metals Portfolio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information you enter into the app stays on your device.

### What the app stores locally on your device:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Holdings information | Track your gold and silver | Device only |
| Price alerts | Notify you of price targets | Device only |
| App preferences | Remember your settings | Device only |

### What the app does NOT collect:

The app does not collect your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information. There are no accounts to create and no sign-ups required.

## Third-Party Services

The app fetches real-time precious metals prices and news from external APIs. These requests do not include any personal information or device identifiers. The app simply requests current gold and silver prices, the same data available on any financial website.

## Data Security

Since all your data remains on your device, you maintain complete control over it. If you delete the app, all stored data is permanently removed. There is no cloud backup or sync feature.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
